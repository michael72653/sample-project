Introducing TAFF

Thank a Firefighter Foundation

When trying to develop a tribute to the firefighters of America by designing a calendar/table top book of firefighting history, stories and acknowledgment, an amazing fact was realized by those involved. There were company organizations and groups that provide education, safety training, needed equipment and the many supplies and products that firefighters need.

There were also foundations and projects that benefited those firefighters who give their time, their health, and for some even their lives. No entity existed to develop and promote activites for Americans to serve, honor and show appreciation to the active serving firefighters of our country... until now...

We proudly present to you TAFF

Providing you, our citizens, and our businesses a way to say THANK YOU to the dedicated men and women of the firefighting service of America.

Potential Objectives

Establish a state, county and city Thank a Firefighter Foundation volunteer network

  • American citizens
  • Businesses
  • Firefighters spouses
  • Civic groups
  • Retired firefighters

Assist in meeting unfilled needs of those injured in the line of duty

  • Develop programs to "Fill the Gap" for firefighter families when and if needed.
  • Promote and establish additional projects and programs for firefighters

Develop and promote nationwide guidelines for "Thank a Firefighter" programs on a city, county and state level

  • TAFF's national firefighter week
  • Fundraising concerts, rodeos and events for fire stations
  • School "Thank a Firefighter Day" at a fire station
  • Take a firefighter to lunch day

Provide a tribute to firefighters called "Dedicated", a calendar/table top book product

  • Firefighters history, Monthly tribute winners and Stories from across America
  • Savings on personal firefighters related items
  • A collectible publication for firefighters
  • Goal is to provide firefighters with this product at no cost

Develop a website for firefighters to share unique information and communicate across the nation

  • Firefighters will contribute details, stories, articles and interesting comments
  • Firefighters will use this website to nominate their nominees for Firefighter, Chief, Chaplain and Station of the week, month, and year honors.

Start and maintain a nationwide email newsletter and/or magazine

  • Promote TAFF projects
  • Recognize and support the FCFI, the Barnabas House project and other firefighter related programs
  • Promote citizen and business involvement in all TAFF activities

Special projects of awards, gifts and vacations

  • Contests and drawings for free products from sponsors
  • Vacation specials, related programs and events
  • Possible scholarship programs for the children of firefighters
